About the Band

Formed in the quiet isolation of endless rainy nights, Lulu Suicide is a dream-pop/shoegaze collective that channels sorrow into walls of beautiful noise. Drawing inspiration from the quietest moments of dawn, their music is an embrace for those who wander alone.


Influences: Cocteau Twins, Slowdive, My Bloody Valentine, Beach House.
